About Us

Pixel & People is a brain child of Three Enthusiatic NIFT Alumni with a common vision to design for the better tomorrow.

We believe in the power of design to solve problems and create meaningful experiences. Our team is composed of seasoned professionals with diverse backgrounds in design, research, and branding. Together, we work to deliver results that not only meet but exceed our clients' expectations.

Our Story

It was May 1st, 2024. Layoffs echoed like thunder, and fear hung heavy in the air. Three recent graduates, Aditi, Sukhmani, and Yashika, stood on the precipice of a world hesitant to value their potential. Yet, in their two years of friendship, they had witnessed each other's brilliance firsthand. Refusing to be defined by circumstance, a spark ignited. Pixel & People, a design studio born from their dreams and determination, bloomed. They wouldn't let the world decide their worth. They would design their own happiness, one joyful pixel at a time.
